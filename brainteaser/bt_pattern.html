Pattern 1

6,6,12,18,40,?

Answer is 58. This is a fibonnacci sequance that starts at 6 instead of 1. Each term is the sum of the previous two numbers in the sequance.

Pattern 2

3,5,9,15,23,?

Answer is 33. Add 2 to the first term, add 4 to the second term, add 6 to the third term and so on until you add 10 to the fifth term.

Pattern 5

6,12,9,18,15,30,?

The answer is 27. You double 6 to get 12 and take off 3 to get 9. This process is repeated until we take 3 off 30 to get 27.

Pattern 6

2,20,4,10,6,5,8,?

2.5. There are two interwoven sequences. 2,4,6,8 and 20,10,5,2.5.

Pattern 7

100,97,91,79,?

The answer is 55. The difference between subsequent terms goes like 3, 3x2=6, 6x2=12, 12x2=24.

Pattern 7

12345, 3579, 81216, 9337, 12610, ?

The answer is 3871. Add each digit to the next digit to get the digit (or digits) in the next number. For example, to get 3579: 1+2=3 2+3=5 3+4=7 4+5=9. To get the answer (3871) we have: 1+2=3 2+6=8 6+1=7 1+0=1.
